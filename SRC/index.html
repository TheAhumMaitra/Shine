About Shine

I created "Shine" for my beloved mother — a heartfelt gift for Mother's Day 2025. It's more than just a webpage; it's a piece of my love and appreciation. The name "Shine" came to me — maybe randomly, or maybe from a song, perhaps the Queen of Tears OST on Spotify. Either way, I instantly fell in love with the name and knew it was perfect. This website took me around 4 hours (maybe a bit more or less) to build. I'm still working on adding more features, even as you read this! I used my current web development skills and started building Shine around 9:33 PM IST. Feel free to check the source code to see how it was made.
